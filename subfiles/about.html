About Us

Approx 15000 tons of plastic is generated in India every day. From it, only 14% of total plastic packaging is getting recycled. Apart from this 95% of plastic packaging value is lost only after single use due to the improper disposal on the grounds of mother earth. This way we are on the edge of losing our own culture being born in the land of great history. Nowadays humankind is adapted to the technical advancements brought in by the corporate world. We see many industries using a fresh woods and water of the planet from different corners in order to produce the tableware of our everyday needs.

Healthy Bharat has perfectly come up with a solution to produce the tableware for running our day-to-day lives with zero wastage and 100% productivity. We manufacture a wide range of biodegradable products like plates, cups, spoons, bowls and trays. We promote not only to make our world better together by using organic products but also in a step to protect both our state of mental and well-being by preserving the nature indirectly.

We aim to bring back the ethics that are taught by our age old sages and saints. This could only happen at the lowest possible price by using our Healthy Bharat products. Our main motto is to educate the citizens of our country and empower them to build a better environment for our children to grow in.

Why Healthy Bharat?

We at “Healthy Bharat” creating and promoting Areca Palm leaf products to reduce the impact on our environment which is natural and biodegradable

The Palm leaf products are being produced with sustainable resource (made from fallen leaves, 100% natural) through eco-friendly process (no chemicals or additives used) and make sure it is acceptable to Earth when disposed (biodegradable and compostable)
