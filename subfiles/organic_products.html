Organic Ghee

Organic ghee

Any meal in India isn't complete without a spoon of desi ghee. Roti, Dal Chawal, Khichadi or Parathas! All need their dollop of Ghee to become complete. And this tradition of serving ghee with the food is present in whole India. From the hills within the north to the bay within the south, every traditional meal requires ghee. As children, we always grew up eating homemade ghee which was made using the standard Vedic process. The delicious taste and also the aroma that filled the house when ghee was getting used to be divine! a decent spoonful of fresh desi ghee poured over piping hot dal chawal could be a favourite meal of the many Indians even today.
